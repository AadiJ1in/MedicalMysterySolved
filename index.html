Our Mission

We exist to make medicine less of a mystery — for patients, students, and communities everywhere.

Medicine is full of ambiguity, and too often, the people who bear the cost of that ambiguity are the ones with the least access to answers. Medical Mystery Solved was built to change that. Through education, global collaboration, and hands-on community work, we connect people to the medical knowledge they deserve — and build the pathways that let the next generation carry that work forward.

What We Do

Education. Outreach. Connection.

Every program we run comes back to the same idea: medical knowledge should not be a privilege. Here is how we put that into practice.

Medical Education

We partner with medical schools and universities to expand access to quality medical instruction. That means co-developing curriculum, bringing in educators, and creating programs that actually reach the people who need them.

Global Partnerships

We build lasting relationships with health organizations, hospitals, and research institutions around the world — creating a network where knowledge flows freely across borders.

Community Outreach

Our chapters host workshops, donation drives, and conversations with healthcare professionals — meeting communities where they are and making medicine feel less distant.

Awareness & Publishing

Members write and publish articles on our platform covering diseases, prevention, and treatment — turning complex medical topics into content that anyone can understand and use.

Health Literacy

We work to reduce stigma and improve how people understand and talk about their health — so that more people can advocate for themselves and their families with confidence.

Events & Collaboration

From local health fairs to inter-chapter panels, our chapters plan and host events that bring people together — and keep the conversation around medicine active in every community we touch.

International Chapters

One organization. Many communities.

MMS chapters are rooted in their local communities and connected to each other. Together, we share resources, plan events, and make sure that the work happening in one part of the world can strengthen the work happening everywhere else.

How It Works

Locally rooted, globally connected

Each chapter runs its own programming — workshops, outreach events, tutoring sessions — tailored to what their community actually needs. But no chapter operates in isolation. We stay in regular communication to share what is working and build on each other's momentum.

Inter-Chapter

Cross-chapter events and planning

Chapters regularly coordinate on joint initiatives — guest speaker series, shared campaigns, and collaborative events that bring multiple communities together around a single goal. The network makes all of us stronger.

Growing

New chapters, new reach

MMS is actively expanding into new schools, cities, and countries. Every new chapter means a new community with access to medical education and a new voice contributing to the broader mission.

University Partners

Medical school partnerships

We partner directly with medical schools to co-develop programming and give their students the chance to contribute as educators and mentors. It is a two-way relationship — everyone learns something.

New Initiative

Tutoring the next generation of healers.

There is no shortage of talented students who want to pursue medicine. What there is a shortage of is opportunity. Our tutoring platform connects students from low-income communities with educators from our network — completely free of charge.

We believe the future of medicine looks like the full diversity of the world it serves. That starts with making sure the path to a medical career is open to everyone, not just those who can afford it.

  • Free, one-on-one tutoring for students in grades 6–12
  • Focus on STEM, biology, chemistry, and pre-med prep
  • Mentorship from university students passionate about medicine
  • College application and personal statement guidance
  • Virtual sessions accessible from any device, anywhere
  • Multilingual support and culturally responsive educators

Meet the Team

Driven by purpose.

The people behind MMS come from different backgrounds, but they share the same conviction — that medical education should be something everyone has access to, and that it is worth showing up every day to make that real.
